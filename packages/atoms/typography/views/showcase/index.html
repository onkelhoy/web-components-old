Typestyles Heading Headings are at the highest level of the typography hierarchy. They are the most prominent style of type. Title Titles are not as prominent or as large as headings. They are used as titles for subsections of a page. Note that Title 4 is in all-caps. Copy Copy styles are at the lowest level of the typography hierarchy. They are used for body text. Copy 3 is the primary style for default body text.
